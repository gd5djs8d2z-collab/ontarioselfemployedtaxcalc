Privacy Policy

How this site handles your information

Last updated: June 2026

About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how OntarioSelfEmployedTaxCalc.ca handles information when you visit this website. This site is a fully static website hosted on Cloudflare Pages with no backend server, no database, and no user account system.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information through this website. We do not collect your name, email, revenue, expenses, income, or any financial figures you enter into the calculator. No data you enter is ever transmitted to any server operated by us.

Calculator Data

All calculator inputs and outputs are processed entirely within your browser using JavaScript. When you close or refresh the page, all entered values are permanently discarded.

Cookies and Analytics

This site does not set any first-party cookies. Google AdSense may set third-party cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can manage Google ad settings at adssettings.google.com or opt out through your browser's privacy settings. Blocking cookies will not affect calculator functionality.

We may use anonymised, aggregated analytics (such as page view counts) to understand how the site is used. No individually identifiable data is collected or retained through analytics.

Third-Party Services

This site uses Google AdSense for advertising and Cloudflare for hosting and CDN delivery. Each may collect standard web data (such as IP addresses and request data) under their own privacy policies. We do not access, control, or retain individual visitor data from these services.

Your Privacy Rights

Under PIPEDA and applicable Canadian privacy law, you have the right to request access to or correction of any personal information held about you. Because this site does not collect personal information through its own systems, there is typically no personal data held by us to access or correct.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. Continued use of this site following any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
