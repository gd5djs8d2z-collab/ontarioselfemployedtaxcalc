Privacy Policy

How this site handles your information

Last updated: June 2026

No Personal Data Collected

OntarioSelfEmployedTaxCalc.ca does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All calculator inputs — revenue, expenses, income, RRSP — are processed entirely within your browser using client-side JavaScript. No data you enter is ever sent to a server.

No Account or Registration

This site requires no account, login, or registration. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.

Cookies

This site does not set first-party cookies. Third-party cookies may be set by Google AdSense (see below) for advertising personalisation purposes.

Google AdSense

This site displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show personalised ads based on your browsing activity. You can review and manage Google's advertising settings at adssettings.google.com. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Analytics

We may use anonymised, aggregated analytics (such as page view counts) to understand how the site is used. No individually identifiable data is collected or retained through analytics.

External Links

This site links to external websites including Calc-HQ.ca and other calculators in the network. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies independently.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of this site following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, visit our Contact page.
